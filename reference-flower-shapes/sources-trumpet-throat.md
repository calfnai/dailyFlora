## Notes for Codex continuation

When continuing the 80-sample set, do not scrape blindly. Each candidate should be evaluated by view type and usefulness:

- front close-up: rim, cup depth, stamens, six tepals
- side profile: trumpet length, flare, connection angle
- three-quarter: spatial relationship between cup and tepals
- dissected/anatomy: tube, ovary, stamens, style
- plant habit: leafless scape, strap leaves, papery spathe
- cultivar variation: short cup, long trumpet, reflexed tepals, rim color
